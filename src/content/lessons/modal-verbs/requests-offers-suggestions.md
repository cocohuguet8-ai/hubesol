## Requests, offers and suggestions

| Function | Patterns | Example |
|---|---|---|
| Request (ask someone) | Can/Could/Would you…? | **Could you** help me? |
| Offer (I do it) | Shall I…? / Can I…? / I'll… | **Shall I** carry that? |
| Suggestion (we do it) | Shall we…? / Let's… / Why don't we…? / How about + -ing? | **Why don't we** eat out? |

*Could* and *would* are more polite than *can*.

## When to use each

### 1. Requests — ask someone to do something

- **Could you** turn the music down? **Would you** mind waiting?

### 2. Offers — offer to do something yourself

- **Shall I** open the window? **I'll** get the door.

### 3. Suggestions — propose doing it together

- **Let's** take a break. **How about** meeting at six? **Why don't we** share a taxi?

> ⚠️ Match the form: **How about** + -ing (*How about going?*) but **Why don't we** + base verb (*Why don't we go?*).

## Common mistakes

**1. 'How about' + base verb.** *~~How about go out?~~* → **How about going out?**

**2. 'Let's' + to.** *~~Let's to start~~* → **Let's start.**

**3. Using 'Shall I' for a suggestion together.** *~~Shall I we go?~~* → **Shall we go?** (offer = Shall I; suggestion = Shall we). **Pro tip:** request = Could/Would you; offer = Shall I; suggestion = Shall we / Let's / Why don't we.
